Wand and Staff

Overview

Wand and Longbow PvE build which provides excellent healing capabilities with a bit of damage dealing when necessary. Our main hand weapon is the Wand, complemented by the Longbow in the off-hand. While the Longbow may seem like an unusual choice, it possesses surprising healing capabilities, which will be elaborated on in the skill section of this build.

Atributes

Strength 19
Dexterity 10
Wisdom 30
Perception 30

Your priority with this build should be Wisdom, as it grants you high max mana which is very needed for a healer. Not only that but Wisdom also improves your mana regeneration and cooldown speed which are all needed. Once Wisdom is at 30, you want to put 30 points into Perception for extra damage which scales up the healing done by your healing abilities. Rest of 9 points can go into Strength for higher base HP that will improve your survivability, improving your max HP
